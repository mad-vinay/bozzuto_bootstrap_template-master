Discover all the amazing features of homztalk. Whether you are a customer, a professional, a supplier, academic staff or a student this is the right place for you to find, learn, discuss and share the ideas.

Learn how it works

Infinite Resources and Ideas

A platform crafted for making your dream design concept to come true. We have made a common platform for the Professionals, Suppliers, Customers, Academic Staffs and students. Here you can learn, find, discuss, design and share your concepts with others to evolve your dream design. Be a part of the most resourceful professional network and experience the power of sharing!

Get the help you need.

Get in touch with all the category of professionals in architecture, design and construction industry. Choose the right professionals by reviewing their profile. Once you deal with the professionals you can write a review on them as well as rate them.

Pick up a Store today.

The suppliers listed will be having wide range of materials which suits your design and construction requirements. Select the best suppliers assuring the quality at the best rates. Find the best store in your locality too!

Learn all you want.

The ideas are designed to know the latest trends, featured designs or construction techniques. A discussion forum will have a healthy debate or discussion on the topics related to design and construction industry. The students can upload their projects/works and the same can be viewed for analyzing yours with that of others. Withstand the future competence!

Take your needed knowledge.

The homz library will be having a wide collection of books, seminar topics, lecture notes, Magazines etc to give the knowledge backup for all categories especially the students in building design/construction. You can also contribute to the homz library by uploading yours. A publisher can upload their magazines/books too!

Choose your design, erase barriers.

Interior and exterior is the showcase of the fabulous designs by various professionals. Get inspiration from the professional designs all over. Make uniqueness in yours, Let the world knows your design.

Calculate and compare.

The hestimator tools are designed to calculate your home estimate in simple steps. It also helps you to Find and compare the best home loans in your country. You can also see the amount to be paid for your home loan at a particular rate of interest. Plan ahead for your dream.
